Aunt May's

Aunt May's is our on premises bakery! Found in the red barn just steps away from our bountiful fields, we sell both pre-picked fruit and delicious baked goods. Find apple cider donuts, peach ginger scones, peach pies, peach jams, and so much more.

Don't have time to pick your own fruit? You can just stop by Aunt May's!

Uncle Bo's Tractor Rides

Visiting with small children, elderly family members, or just want to ride on a tractor? You are in luck! Uncle Bo loves to drive the tractor and gives our visitors a tour of our peach orchards on the hour, every hour, every day we're open (Monday-Saturday 8am-5pm).
